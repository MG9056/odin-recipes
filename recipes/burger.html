Chicken Burger

Description

A Chicken burger consists of fillingsâ€”usually a patty of chicken placed inside a sliced bun or bread roll. The patties are often served with cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, bacon, or chilis with condiments such as ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise.

Ingredients

  • Burger Bun
  • Chicken Patty
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Mayonnaise
  • Cheese slice

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat
  2. Cook patties in hot oil until golden and well browned on the bottoms, 5 to 6 minutes
  3. Turn patties and cook until other sides are browned, 3 to 4 minutes more
  4. Assemble the burger by first placing the bottom bun on the plate
  5. Then add the patty,mayonnaise,cheese slice,lettuce,tomatoes,onions in order
  6. Complete the burger by adding the top bun and serve
